Questions & Answers

1. Why is the viewport meta tag important?

It makes web pages look good on all screen sizes, like phones, tablets, and computers.

2. How can you adjust the CSS for printing?

Use @media print to change styles for printing, like hiding menus and making text black.

3. When would you want to adjust the CSS for printing?

When you need a clean, easy-to-read printout without extra images, colors, or navigation menus.
