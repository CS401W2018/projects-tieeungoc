Positioning in CSS

  • What is normal flow in HTML? It is the default way elements appear on a webpage, one after another.
  • What does float do? Why clear float? Float moves elements to the left or right. Clearing float prevents layout issues.
  • Absolute, fixed, and relative positions? Absolute puts it in a set spot, fixed keeps it in place when scrolling, relative moves it a little from its normal spot.
  • How to make good mobile navigation? Make them big, easy to tap, and spaced out. A menu icon helps.
