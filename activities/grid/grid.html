What should you focus on during class and after class?
During class: Listen, take notes, and ask questions.
After class: Review notes, practice, and try small exercises.

What is the main difference between CSS Grid and CSS Flex?
Grid: Works in rows and columns, good for layouts.
Flex: Works in one direction (row or column), good for aligning items.
