My Profile Page

Introduction:

Hello! My name is Ngoc, you can call me Nancy. I am 24 years old and from Vietnam, a beautiful country in Asia. My family lives in Ho Chi Minh City, and I have an older brother who works as a mechanical engineer in Utah. In my free time, I like doing nails for my friends, watching horror movies, and cooking Vietnamese food to share with others. I am a sophomore at BYU Hawaii, and I really enjoy the diversity and culture here. I have made great friends and learned a lot about different cultures. Besides my classes, I love joining club activities. I am also the president of the Vietnam Club, where I share my culture and bring people together.

As a student and a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, I have learned many important lessons since my mission. Studying at BYU Hawaii and serving as a leader has taught me the value of hard work, service, and setting a good example. These experiences have helped me grow spiritually, emotionally, and intellectually. I am truly grateful for these opportunities to prepare for a bright future.

More things about me:
