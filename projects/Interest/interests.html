Contemporary dance

Contemporary dance is a dynamic and expressive dance style that blends elements of modern, ballet, and jazz dance. It focuses on fluid movements, floor work, and emotional expression, often using improvisation and abstract storytelling.

Hip-hop

Hip-hop started in New York City in the 1970s as a culture with rapping, DJing, breakdancing, and graffiti. Its dance style is freestyle and full of energy, with moves like popping, locking, and breaking. It focuses on rhythm, groove, and self-expression, making it popular in battles, performances, and TikTok trends.

Modern dance

Modern dance is a free and expressive style that started in the early 1900s. It focuses on natural movements, using the whole body and emotions. Dancers use techniques like contract-release, floor work, and falling and rising. It is creative and lets dancers move in their own way.
