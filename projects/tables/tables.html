Who is Keshi?

Keshi (Casey Luong) is a Vietnamese-American singer-songwriter and producer. Known for his unique blend of R&B, lo-fi, and alternative music, he gained popularity through SoundCloud before signing with a major label.

His debut album, GABRIEL, marked a turning point in his career, showcasing his growth as an artist.
