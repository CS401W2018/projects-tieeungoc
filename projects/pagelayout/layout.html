My Favorite K-Drama: Crash Landing on You

Home K-Dramas Actors

Crash Landing on You is one of the most popular K-Dramas of all time. It tells the unexpected love story between a South Korean heiress and a North Korean officer. The show gained huge international success, making it the best for drama lovers.

The story of Yoon Se-ri, a fearless South Korean businesswoman who accidentally paraglides into North Korea**. There, she meets Ri Jeong-hyeok, a dedicated officer who helps her return home. Their journey is filled with romance, action, and heartfelt moments.

  • Yoon Se-ri – A confident South Korean heiress who unexpectedly lands in North Korea.
  • Ri Jeong-hyeok – A kind but serious North Korean officer who helps Se-ri escape.
  • Seo Dan – A wealthy woman in North Korea with her own love story.
  • Gu Seung-jun – A mysterious con artist who finds unexpected love.
