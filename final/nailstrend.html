✨ 2025 Nail Trends ✨

Explore the hottest nail styles this year - from futuristic finishes to dreamy Korean inspired art.

Why Update Nail Trends everyday?

Nail art is more than just beauty. It’s about expressing who you are. Whether you are into soft, romantic vibes or bold, eye-catching glam, 2025 has so many exciting styles to explore. At Nancy Nails Studio, we’ll help you find the perfect look that fits your mood and personality.

✨ Mirror Chrome

Shiny and futuristic, chrome nails are great for nights out or events. Popular in silver, gold, or pink chrome.

🌈 Aura Nails

Soft color blends around the center of each nail create a peaceful, dreamy vibe, and they look even more magical with a touch of sparkle!

💎 3D Charms

Tiny bows, flowers, sparkly gems, and fun shapes bring your nails to life using builder gel. It’s an extra touch of glam that's perfect for birthdays, photoshoots, or just treating yourself!

🌸 Korean Style Nails

Korean nails are soft and minimal, using sheer polish, tiny stickers, and sparkles for a clean and cute look.

🍬 Jelly Gel Nails

Jelly gel nails are bright, see-through, and totally look like candy! They're fun, glossy, and perfect for summer, especially with cute fruit charms.

🎀 3D Gel Nail Art

These designs use thick gel to create ribbons, hearts, and cute shapes that pop off your nails, just like the ones Korean idols wear!

Tips for Beauty

  • 💡 Chrome nails look amazing with minimalist rings to make them shine more.
  • 🧴 Use cuticle oil daily to keep your 3D charms and sculpted nails healthy.
  • 🎨 Match your aura or jelly nails with outfits, hair color, or the season.
